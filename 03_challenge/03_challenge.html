Ugali Recipe

Ingredients lists

  • 5 cups of maize flour
  • 8 cups of water

Cooking Steps

  1. Turn on the stove
  2. Place the pot on the stove.
  3. Put the 8 cups of water in the pot
  4. Gradually add the maize flour while stirring continuously.
  5. Continue stirring and cooking until the mixture becomes smooth until it's ready.

Nutritional Facts

Nutrient Amount
Carbohydrates 38gramm
Protein 2gramm
Fat 5gramm

Note: Ugali is best served while its hot.
